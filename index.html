Hi. I'm Ciara McEntee.

A photography student at Dublin Institute of Technology. I specialize in romantic and artistic portraiture photography with a hint of modernity. I love to take pictures of people & cultural environments. My goal is to show the beauty, grace, strength and enthusiasm of people and the world.

Learn about my studies

Dublin Institute of Technology

DT559 - Bachelor of Arts (Hons) Photography

Critical Studies

Incorporating Visual Studies, History, Theory and Criticism. This area enables students to analyse and account for the ways in which photography permeates all spheres of human experience and activity.

Photographic Practice

Exploring all aspects of photographic practice, including the genres of photography; the range of image formats; meeting or subverting audience expectations; the contexts in which photographic media operate.

Professional Studies

Gaining a solid foundation in the concepts, tools, and techniques required to become a self-employed professional photographer. Continuous experience has since enhanced my skill within the industry.
